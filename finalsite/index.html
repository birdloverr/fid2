Join us! Recruiting new members for our Floral Arrangements Club.

June 20, 2022

The Floral Arrangements Club at the Sakura Floral Society welcomes you! We are looking for new members who are passionate about bouquets and all types of floral arrangements.
We meet Monday evenings in the SFS crafts room. If interested, please contact Shirley at shirley@sakurafloral.com.

Free bouquet making - open to the public!

April 2nd, 2022

This week, the Floral Arrangements Club at the Sakura Floral Society is hosting a free bouquet-making event! Held this Wednesday, the event is open to the general public at no cost.
Each person may make their own bouquet from a range of flowers available! For questions or comments, please contact events@sakurafloral.com.
